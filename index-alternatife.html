Our Work

“Batubulan” Photograph Series

This series is a homage to the memories of the artist’s traditional home in Batubulan, Bali in form of botanical arrangement. Bushy greeneries, water lilies, and abundant tropical fruits sourced from the house’s garden and local market are set on some familiar home objects including the house’s old wooden furniture pieces.

“Not Your Typical Romance” Photograph Series

A fusion love story between wild local goodness and delicate blue delphiniums set against a classic European aesthetic. Adding a touch of drama with ronce melati, the infamous jasmine garland commonly seen in Javanese traditional weddings and banana flowers curled around classic ornamented stair railing

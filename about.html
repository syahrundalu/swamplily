About

Initially grew from a deep interest in the botanical world and storytelling, Swamp Lily exists as a medium to connect the two. We believe that flowers and plants, like any other piece of nature, possess a raw, delicate, and sentimental quality which becomes our main perspective in creating. Our creative approach is mostly inspired by the idea of everything in between—a mix of something quirky, unexpected, and precious.
